What is Agentic AI?
Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that can take actions autonomously, pursue goals, and exhibit initiative, often making decisions without human intervention.

What is Groq?
Groq is a hardware and AI inference platform that provides ultra-fast LLM response times using OpenAI-compatible APIs.

How do I build a chatbot?
To build a chatbot, you can use tools like LangChain, vector databases, embedding models, and a language model backend like OpenAI or Groq.
